About us

Your online meal planner..

Welcome to our meal inspiration app! Search a database of recipes to get inspiration based on your meal preferences. You can enter any intolerances, special diet requirements, the type of cuisine you’d like to try and how much time you’d like to allow to prepare the meal- then hit search and see the meal suggestions! You can cycle through the results and ‘favourite’ the ones you’d like. Then head over to the favourite’s page whenever you want, and all of your favourite recipes will be saved, with a link to the ingredients required and cooking instructions. You can create a meal plan for the week, or just save recipes to try when you want. The culinary world is at your fingertips, we hope you find some inspiration and enjoy!
